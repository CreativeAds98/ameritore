SHELL FABS Factory Building

Industrial Project

We are pleased to present the SHELL FABS Factory Building in Kumurapuram, consisting of a 3,350 sq. ft. factory shed and an ancillary plus office building of 2,300 sq. ft. (lean roof). The factory features a clear span size of 81.6 ft x 41.2 ft x 16.5 ft (clear height), designed for maximum operational efficiency. This project integrates functional industrial space with modern office facilities, reflecting our commitment to high-quality construction and versatile design.
