About Us

Shaping the Future of Infrastructure

Founded in 2003 as Jayin Associates, Ameritore Infrastructure is a leading provider of end-to-end infrastructure solutions across industrial, commercial, and residential sectors. With expertise in civil engineering and pre-engineered building (PEB) solutions, we design and construct high-performance structures, IT-enabled office spaces, and advanced PEB facilities equipped with HVAC systems, industrial lighting, and automation.

Driven by a skilled team and strategic industry collaborations, we prioritize efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and timely project execution. Our commitment to innovation and quality ensures the creation of functional, visually striking spaces that deliver long-term value for our clients.

Our Services

COMMERCIAL BUILDING

At Ameritore Infrastructure, we recognize that commercial spaces must be functional, visually appealing, and designed for business success. Our comprehensive construction solutions cater to retail stores, office buildings, restaurants, and more, ensuring each project is customized to meet the client’s unique requirements. With a strong focus on budget, design, and sustainability, we create spaces that not only enhance productivity but also elevate customer experiences and align with your brand’s identity. Whether it’s a corporate headquarters or a boutique retail outlet, we deliver high-quality, purpose-driven environments that support growth and innovation.

RESIDENTIAL BUILDINGS

At Ameritore Infrastructure, we specialize in crafting residential spaces that seamlessly blend superior craftsmanship with modern design. From elegant single-family homes to expansive apartment complexes, we prioritize comfort, safety, and sustainability in every project. Whether it's a bespoke home or a large-scale residential development, our client-centric approach ensures tailored solutions that meet unique lifestyle needs. With a commitment to high-quality materials and expert execution, we create durable, timeless homes that offer lasting value and exceptional living experiences.

CIVIL STRUCTURAL BUILDING

At Ameritore Infrastructure, we are dedicated to constructing robust civil structures that enhance communities and improve public life. With extensive experience in developing schools, hospitals, government buildings, and civic infrastructure, we ensure every project is built to the highest standards of safety, durability, and compliance. Our team collaborates closely with local authorities to secure necessary permits and approvals, ensuring smooth execution within budget and timeline constraints. By prioritizing functionality, longevity, and community well-being, we create infrastructure that serves generations to come.

ARCHITECTURAL & INTERIOR DESIGN

At Ameritore Infrastructure, we believe that exceptional design is the cornerstone of every successful project. Our architectural and interior design services focus on creating spaces that seamlessly combine functionality, aesthetics, and innovation. By integrating visionary architecture with modern interior design, we craft environments that are not only visually striking but also enhance user experience and practicality. Whether designing contemporary commercial spaces or elegant residential interiors, we bring creativity and precision to every project, transforming spaces into inspiring and functional masterpieces.

EXTENSIONS & RENOVATION

At Ameritore Infrastructure, we specialize in transforming spaces to maximize their potential. Whether updating an outdated property, expanding a home, or modifying a commercial space to meet evolving demands, our expert team ensures seamless integration of design, functionality, and aesthetics. From concept to completion, we handle every aspect—including design, permits, construction, and finishing touches—ensuring that your renovation or extension enhances the existing structure while improving usability and visual appeal. Let us bring your vision to life with innovative solutions that elevate your space.

PRE ENGINEERED BUILDING (PEB)

At Ameritore Infrastructure, we deliver cost-effective, efficient, and sustainable Pre-Engineered Building (PEB) solutions tailored for industrial and commercial applications. Designed for flexibility and rapid installation, our PEB structures minimize maintenance while maximizing durability and functionality. Whether for warehouses, factories, or storage facilities, our customizable designs ensure optimal space utilization and energy efficiency. Using high-quality steel and advanced materials, we create robust, long-lasting structures that meet industry standards while offering superior performance and sustainability.

Mechanical, Electrical & Plumbing

At Ameritore Infrastructure, we provide comprehensive Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing (MEP) solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. Our expert team ensures seamless integration of electrical, lighting, and plumbing systems that enhance efficiency, safety, and sustainability. We specialize in reliable electrical wiring, energy-efficient lighting installations, and system upgrades that meet industry standards. Our plumbing services guarantee a smooth water supply and efficient drainage using high-quality materials to prevent leaks and disruptions. From design and installation to maintenance and troubleshooting, we deliver long-lasting, high-performance solutions to keep your property running efficiently.

Valued Added Service

At Ameritore Infrastructure, we go beyond conventional construction solutions to offer specialized value-added services that simplify and enhance your project experience. From navigating complex regulatory approvals to ensuring financial transparency, our expertise helps streamline the process, saving you time and effort. We work closely with you at every stage, providing tailored support to make your journey seamless and stress-free. Our commitment to efficiency, compliance, and client satisfaction ensures that your project is executed with precision and ease.

Building Approval & Liaoning
